Frequently Asked Questions

How to Play?

Use arrow keys on desktop or on-screen buttons on mobile to control the snake's direction. Collect food to grow and increase your score.

How is Score Calculated?

You earn 10 points for each food item collected. The game speeds up as you collect more food!

What Causes Game Over?

The game ends if you hit the walls or collide with your own tail. Try to plan your moves carefully!

How to Get High Scores?

Focus on efficient movement, plan your path ahead, and try to use all available space as you grow longer.
